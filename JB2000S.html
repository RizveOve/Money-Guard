KINGTON JB-2000S Money Counting Machine

KINGTON JB-2000S Money Counting Machine is especially for heavy-duty counting capable to count loose banknotes or bundle of currency, no need open currency strap highly improve cash processing efficiency.

Technical Specification:

->Feed System: Vacuum Type Feed System
->Counting Speed: Variable: 3, 3.5, 4 Sec./100notes.
->Batched Count: 50, 100 and up to the user needed (from 1-999)
->Display: Super bright and large LED, Count: 3 digits, Batch: 5 digits
->Applicable note: Width: 45-100; Length: 130-240, Thickness: 0.07-0.12mm
->Holder Capacity: Max 200 Sheets
->Counting Mode: Check, Free, Batch, ADD, UV
->Dimension: 350 (W) x 400 (D) x 790 (H) mm
->Weight: N.W. 38 kg, G.W. 40 kg
->Power Supply: AC 110V 60 Hz or 220-240V, 50 Hz
->Power Consumption: Max. 350W, Ampere: 5A
->Ambient Conditions: Temperature 0~40 degree, Humidity 30 ~ 75%
->Optional Device: OK Stamp Module, UV Detection, Remote Display


Features:

High Accuracy

Count Sensor System of JB-2000 Series has been designed on Kington’s high technology and long experience and has an incomparable accuracy in counting.

Long-term Durability

The mechanism of JB-2000 Series with high reliability continues to be selected and highly evaluated by customers in need of the Heavy-duty equipment.

Variable Counting Speed

JB-2000 Series have high speed counting and greatly enhance the customers’ efficiency in their routine works. Variable: 3 Sec./100notes, 3.5 Sec./100notes, 4 Sec./100notes, 4.5 Sec. /100notes, suit to different condition of currencies or coupons.

Easy Operation

Compact and excellent design of JB-2000 Series was made for the operators’ easier usage. JB-2000 Series are equipped with Reset Switches in both left and right hand sides, large and bright LED Display to minimize the operators’ heavy burden.

Versatile Modes

Versatile Modes of JB-2000 Series comply with the customers’ various demands. Check Mode / ADD Count / BATCH / Check Mode + ACC Count/ BATCH+ADD-Count Stamp Mode is also available as an optional kit of Stamping Device.

Counterfeit Detection

JB-2000 Series UV detection designed so that fake notes could be detected & selected out during the counting process. Users can save time from checking counterfeit between friction counter & counterfeit detector. It is capable of counting loose banknote & strap 100pcs banknote without opening the strap.
